Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register as an artist?

You can register as an artist by clicking the "Sign Up" button and selecting "Artist" as your user type. After registration, you can upload your artwork and create auctions.

How do I participate in an auction?

To participate in an auction, you need to create an account as a buyer. Once logged in, you can browse live auctions and place bids on artwork you're interested in.

What are the fees for selling artwork?

We charge a 10% commission on all successful auction sales. There are no listing fees or upfront costs for artists.

How do I receive payment for sold artwork?

Payments are processed through our secure payment system. After an auction ends, the winning bid amount (minus our commission) will be transferred to your account within 7 business days.
