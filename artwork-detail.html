Sunset Over the Mountains

Jane Smith
Boulder, CO
Category Landscape
Size 24" x 36"
Medium Oil on Canvas

This stunning landscape captures the beauty of a sunset over the Rocky Mountains. Painted during a plein air session in Rocky Mountain National Park, this piece showcases the vibrant colors of the evening sky reflected on the mountain peaks.

The painting measures 24" x 36" and is framed in a beautiful walnut frame. The colors are rich and vibrant, with deep purples and oranges in the sky contrasting with the dark silhouettes of the mountains.
